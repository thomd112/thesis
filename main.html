MADHUBANI ART



Madhubani or Mithila painting is a folk art of the Mithila region.

The Mithila region includes the north of Bihar and the eastern Terai of Nepal.

mah-doo-bah-nee


characteristics: 2d figures, double lines no empty spaces, no gaps, border: nature elements or geometrical
